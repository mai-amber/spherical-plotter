3D Data Visualization Tool

Paste your 3D point data in the format: x, y, z (one point per line)


Available Views:

  • Top-Down (X-Y): Shows the data from above, Z-coordinate hidden
  • Side View (X-Z): Shows the data from the side, Y-coordinate hidden
  • Front View (Y-Z): Shows the data from the front, X-coordinate hidden
  • Spherical (φ-θ): Shows angular positions in spherical coordinates
